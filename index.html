Assalamu 'Alaikum wa Rahmatullāhi wa Barākatuh

I am pleased to formally invite you to join COAP | Da'awatul-Rusul, a non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to sharing Islam and reviving the eternal truth that all prophets were sent to convey. Established by the grace of Allāh, our organization operates upon the prophetic methodology of knowledge, sincerity, patience, wisdom, and mercy.

We believe that every prophet—from Adam to Noah, Abraham to Moses, Jesus to Muhammad, peace be upon them all—came with one singular message: the worship of Allāh alone, the upholding of justice, and the invitation to righteousness. Our mission is to carry forward this sacred legacy in our time.

"Who is better in speech than one who calls to Allāh, does righteousness, and says, 'Indeed, I am among the Muslims.'" (Qur'an 41:33)
Sincerely,
Ibrahim Muhammad
